About Valencia Tennis

Valencia Tennis Club is Valencia's premiere tennis venue. Nestled beside the Turia River Park, the 4 clay courts of the club provide year round play for members of all levels. Start your membership today to immediately get access to our courts, competitions, club events and more. We're looking forward to seeing you on the courts.

Fitness

Get the full body workout you need without tedious reps and sets! (OK, maybe there are some sets...) Tennis allows you to get your daily exercise in, all while having fun!

Community

Our club is home to players of all levels who want to enjoy the game and make friends. Playing with us is a great way to get involved with your local community, and we can't wait to meet you!
